Policies

Policies / Terms & Conditions

By scheduling an appointment, you acknowledge and agree to our terms and conditions outlined below. These policies are designed to ensure a seamless, professional, and enjoyable experience for all clients.

Booking

All appointments must be booked in advance through the online booking system or by phone. This allows us to organise our schedule efficiently and provide each client with the highest level of care. Please ensure your contact details are accurate so you can receive booking confirmations, reminders, and any important updates regarding your appointment.

Cancellation

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment may be charged 50% of the service price.

Late Arrival

If you arrive late, your treatment time may be reduced to ensure that the next client is not delayed. The full treatment fee will still apply. We recommend arriving a few minutes early to fully enjoy your session.

Health & Safety

Please inform us of any medical conditions, injuries, or allergies prior to your appointment. This ensures treatments are safe and tailored to your needs.

Payment

Payments are required at the time of booking or immediately after the session. We accept cash and selected electronic payment methods.

No-Show

Clients who do not show up for their appointment without notice may be charged the full service amount. If you’re unable to attend, please let us know as soon as possible so we can offer the time to another client and keep everything running smoothly.

Refund

We kindly inform you that we do not offer refunds for any treatments or gift vouchers once they have been purchased. Please note that all deposits are strictly non-refundable. However, deposits can be applied toward the cost of your treatment. By booking an appointment, you agree to these terms. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

Privacy

Your personal information is kept confidential and used only for booking and communication purposes. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.

Cookies

We use cookies to ensure our website functions properly and to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us analyze traffic and, where applicable, deliver personalized content or advertising. Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use. You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.
